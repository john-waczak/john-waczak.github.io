About me

I am a physics graduate student at the Unversity of Texas at Dallas. I have a wide range of interestes invovling computational physics, relativity, optics, differential geometry, and many others. This June I graduated suma cum laude from Oregon State University with two bachelor of science degrees in physics and mathematics. I wrote my undergaduate thesis on a two-dimensional model for the dynein motor protein and am looking forward to getting started with research at UTD.

I plan to use this website to host information about what I'm doing as well as interesting physics & mathematics, and ocassionally, will post cool deep sky photographs.
